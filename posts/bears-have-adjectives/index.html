Bears have adjectives

Published on 2020-02-01 by Emma Juettner

Transcription

Please read!
Bears have adjectives,
Sun-drenched
transporting, hypnotically beautiful
Magnificent
Glowing!
in a floral state of mind
pretty
Uncondemned
of cosmic proportions!
